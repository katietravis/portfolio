1) Introduction

Pantala aims to make traveling less of a hassle for hardworking parents in need of family time, away from work time, self-care time, or all of the above.

2) Research

Potential users were surveyed on their travel habits. User insights included emphasis on family activities, organization, inspiration, and affordability.

3) Challenge

This case study aims to address the convoluted and inefficient travel booking process in a way that is accessible and doesn't break the bank.

4) Ideation

Value adding features include an interactive map, saved favorites feature, an itinerary feature with alert capability, a community chat, and a budget tool.

5) Prototyping

During the prototyping phase, colors were added to generate excitement and exude sophistication. The onboarding process was mapped out as well.

6) Conclusion

Users were able to successfully navigate the app during testing. Future development will require consistently updated listings for activities, eateries, and lodging.
