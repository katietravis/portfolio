1) Introduction

Lemonzest is a recipe app for organizing meal prep, locating local ingredients, and making healthful meal decisions that won't break the bank.

2) Research

We asked potential users about their eating and meal prepping habits. Emphasis on healthful eating, timely meals, and budget dominated the responses.

4) Ideation

Features include meal categories based on dietary preferences, budget, and ingredients, along with an onboarding questionnaire.

5) Prototyping

The onboarding questionnaire was mapped out to provide users a customized search experience with options for dietary restrictions and difficulty level.

6) Conclusion

Testing was successful, with some users opting out of the questionnaire to peruse recipes. Future development may include new meal categories.
